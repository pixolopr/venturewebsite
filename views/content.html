Something about us...

Venture Startups is a "not for profit" organization registered in 2014. It is based at Hyderabad.
Venture startups is a platform to empower the willing entrepreneur with both skills and resources to ensure the success of his mission. We recognize the immense potential of working professionals and enterprising students, who seek to channel their intellectual and technological potential for economic benefit. And we understand the social, economic, and cultural context that influences the exploration. Consequently, we propose a platform that nurtures the ideas, provides the resources, and trains in the skills that culminate in entrepreneurship. For working professionals, this platform acts as a mid-stage whereas for students, this platform acts as an incubator.

Who We Are? Meet Our Team!

Venture Startups is promoted by visionaries, entrepreneurs, and technocrats. The chief promoter is Mr. R. Ramkumar. Mr. Ramkumar holds an MBA in HRD, LLM in Buiness Laws, and qualified for Ph.D in Microeconomics from BITS. He is a polyglot, who lived in India, US, Singapore, and the Middle East. He is based out of Mumbai, India. Ms. Anita Sakuru, our co-promoter, is a technology graduate from BITS and Kellogs School of Management. She promoted successful IT companies in India and the US. She is based in Bangalore. Mr. Yogesh Bala, our co-promoter, is a professional architect. He is a successful entrepreneur with 23 years of track record. He lives in Hyderabad.
